Hainanese Chicken Rice

Poached chicken served with fragrant rice and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • Garlic, ginger

Steps

  1. Poach chicken gently; reserve broth. Cook rice in broth with garlic & ginger.
  2. Slice chicken; serve with soy-ginger and chili sauces.

Chili Crab

Sweet-spicy crab in a thick tomato-chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 kg fresh crab
  • Tomato sauce, chilies, egg

Steps

  1. Stir-fry sauce ingredients; add crab and simmer until cooked; finish with beaten egg for silkiness.

Laksa (Katong)

Coconut curry noodle soup.

Ingredients

  • Laksa paste, coconut milk, rice noodles, prawns

Steps

  1. Cook paste, add coconut milk & stock, add noodles & prawns. Serve with laksa leaves.

Hokkien Mee

Stir-fried yellow noodles with seafood and rich stock.

Steps

  1. Wok-fry seafood and noodles; add concentrated stock to create a glossy finish. Serve with sambal and lime.

Kaya Toast

Toast with kaya (coconut jam) and butter; classic breakfast.

Steps

  1. Toast bread; spread kaya and butter; serve with soft-boiled eggs and coffee.
Serve warm for breakfast.
