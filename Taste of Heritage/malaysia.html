Nasi Lemak

Coconut rice with sambal, anchovies, peanuts, cucumber & egg.

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 pandan leaves
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 200 g dried anchovies
  • 80 g roasted peanuts
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cucumber

Sambal

  • 200 g red chilies
  • 4 shallots
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp tamarind (diluted)
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Steps — rice

  1. Wash rice; add to cooker with coconut milk, water, pandan, salt; cook.
  2. When done, fluff and keep warm.

Steps — sambal

  1. Blend chilies, shallots, garlic into a rough paste.
  2. Fry paste in oil until oil separates and fragrant (~8–10 min).
  3. Add tamarind and sugar, simmer until thick; season to taste.

Steps — assemble

  1. Fry anchovies until crisp. Boil eggs and halve. Slice cucumber.
  2. On a plate: rice, sambal, anchovies, peanuts, egg and cucumber.
Serve warm. Optionally add fried chicken for nasi lemak ayam goreng.

Roti Canai

Flaky layered flatbread—crispy outside, soft inside.

Ingredients (~6)

  • 500 g flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 250–300 ml warm water

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar. Add ghee and water to form dough. Knead 6–8 minutes.
  2. Divide into balls; coat with oil and rest 2+ hours.
  3. Stretch thin, fold into layers, rest 10 min.
  4. Fry on hot pan with oil/ghee until golden both sides.
Serve with dhal or curry.

Laksa (Curry Laksa)

Coconut curry noodle soup with shrimp or chicken.

Ingredients (4)

  • 200 g rice noodles
  • 300 g shrimp or chicken
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 600 ml stock
  • 3 tbsp laksa paste

Steps

  1. Fry laksa paste in oil 2–3 min. Add stock and coconut milk, simmer.
  2. Add protein; cook until done. Blanch noodles separately.
  3. Assemble: noodles, broth, toppings (bean sprouts, coriander, lime).
Serve hot with sambal and lime wedges.

Char Kway Teow

Wok-fried flat rice noodles with seafood and smoky wok-hei.

Ingredients (3–4)

  • 300 g fresh flat rice noodles
  • 200 g prawns
  • 2 Chinese sausages
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Prep sauces and ingredients; have them ready—cooking is fast.
  2. Heat wok very hot; add oil, garlic, then prawns & sausage.
  3. Add noodles and sauces; toss. Push aside, scramble eggs, fold into noodles. Add bean sprouts last.
Serve immediately to enjoy the smoky flavor.

Hainanese Chicken Rice

Silky poached chicken with fragrant rice and ginger-chili sauces.

Ingredients (4)

  • 1 whole chicken (1.4–1.8 kg)
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 4–5 slices ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp chicken fat

Steps — chicken & rice

  1. Poach chicken gently 30–35 min; rest in hot water 15 min; ice bath 5 min. Reserve broth.
  2. Sauté garlic & ginger in chicken fat; add rice and toast 1–2 min, then cook in broth.

Sauces & assembly

  1. Make ginger-spring onion oil and chili sauce (blend chilies, garlic, vinegar, sugar).
  2. Slice chicken; serve with rice, cucumber, sauces and a bowl of clear broth.
Serve warm; the silky skin and fragrant rice are essential.
