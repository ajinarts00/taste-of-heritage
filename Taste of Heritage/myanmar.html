Tradish — Myanmar

Five Burmese dishes with step-by-step instructions.

← Back

Mohinga

Fish-based noodle soup — Myanmar's national dish.

Steps

  1. Make fish broth with aromatics; add rice vermicelli and garnish with fried fritters and herbs.

Lahpet (Tea Leaf Salad)

Fermented tea leaf salad with crunchy toppings.

Steps

  1. Toss fermented tea leaves with peanuts, sesame, garlic oil, tomato and cabbage. Serve as salad.

Shan Noodles

Tomato-based noodle dish from Shan State.

Steps

  1. Prepare tomato-meat sauce; serve over rice noodles with pickles and herbs.

Burmese Curry

Simple curry served with rice.

Steps

  1. Sauté curry paste and onions; add protein and simmer until tender. Serve with rice.

Myanmar-style Samosa

Crispy pastry filled with spiced potato or meat.

Steps

  1. Prepare spiced filling; fill pastry and deep-fry until golden.
Serve warm as snack.
