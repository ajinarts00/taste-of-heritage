Sticky Rice (Khao Niao)

Steamed glutinous rice — Laos staple.

Steps

  1. Soak glutinous rice overnight; steam in bamboo basket until translucent.
Serve warm with savory dishes.

Larb

Minced meat salad with toasted rice powder and herbs.

Steps

  1. Cook minced meat lightly; toss with lime, fish sauce, toasted rice and herbs.
Serve with raw vegetables and sticky rice.

Tom Kha (Laos)

Coconut milk soup with galangal and lemongrass.

Steps

  1. Simmer aromatics in coconut milk; add mushrooms and protein; season and serve.

Khao Piak Sen

Comforting thick rice noodle soup.

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken broth; add flat rice noodles and herbs; serve hot.

Sai Oua (Laotian sausage)

Herb-filled grilled sausage.

Steps

  1. Mix minced pork with herbs and spices, stuff into casings and grill.
Serve sliced with sticky rice and herbs.
