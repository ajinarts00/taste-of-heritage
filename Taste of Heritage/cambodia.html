Amok Trey

Steamed fish in coconut kroeung custard.

Steps

  1. Make kroeung paste; mix with coconut milk and eggs, add fish, steam in banana leaves until set.

Samlor Korko

Hearty vegetable & coconut soup.

Steps

  1. Sauté kroeung, add stock and vegetables, simmer until tender, add protein and season.

Nom Banh Chok

Rice noodles with fish curry gravy.

Steps

  1. Prepare fish-based green curry gravy; strain and pour over rice noodles with fresh herbs.

Bai Sach Chrouk

Grilled marinated pork with rice.

Steps

  1. Marinate thin pork slices in garlic & soy; grill slowly and serve with rice & pickles.

Kuy Teav

Clear rice noodle soup with beef or pork.

Steps

  1. Simmer bones for broth; blanch noodles and top with hot broth, herbs and toppings.
