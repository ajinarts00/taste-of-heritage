Five Vietnamese dishes with step-by-step instructions.

Phở

Aromatic beef or chicken noodle soup.

Steps

  1. Simmer bones with star anise, cinnamon, ginger and onion for hours to make clear aromatic broth.
  2. Serve over rice noodles with herbs and lime.

Bánh Mì

Vietnamese sandwich with pâté, pickles and herbs.

Steps

  1. Slice roll; spread pâté/mayo; add protein, pickled carrot & daikon, cilantro and cucumber.

Gỏi Cuốn (Fresh Spring Rolls)

Rice paper rolls with shrimp/pork and herbs.

Steps

  1. Soften rice paper; place fillings and roll tight. Serve with hoisin-peanut sauce.

Bún Chả

Grilled pork served with rice noodles and dipping sauce.

Steps

  1. Grill pork patties and slices; serve with noodles, herbs and sweet-sour dipping sauce.

Cá Kho Tộ (Caramelized Fish)

Fish caramelized in a claypot-style sauce.

Steps

  1. Caramelize sugar; add fish, fish sauce, pepper and simmer until sauce coats fish.
Serve with steamed rice.
