Nasi Goreng

Indonesian fried rice with sweet soy and egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp kecap manis
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic and shallot; add rice and kecap manis; stir-fry until combined.
  2. Push rice aside, fry egg and top the rice. Serve with pickles.
Serve hot with prawn crackers.

Rendang

Slow-cooked spicy beef in coconut — rich and dry.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • Rendang spice paste (lemongrass, galangal, chilies)

Steps

  1. Brown beef; add paste and coconut milk; simmer on low for 2–3 hours until dry and caramelized.
Serve with steamed rice.

Soto Ayam

Turmeric chicken soup with noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Rice noodles or vermicelli

Steps

  1. Simmer chicken with turmeric and aromatics to make broth. Shred chicken and serve with noodles and fried shallots.
Garnish with lime and herbs.

Gado-Gado

Boiled vegetables with peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • Assorted boiled vegetables
  • Tofu
  • Peanut sauce

Steps

  1. Arrange boiled vegetables and tofu; pour peanut sauce and serve with crackers.

Sate (Satay)

Skewered marinated meat with peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken or beef
  • Marinade: soy, garlic, turmeric
  • Peanut sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate, skewer, grill over charcoal until cooked; serve with peanut sauce and rice cakes.
Serve hot with cucumber-onion pickles.
