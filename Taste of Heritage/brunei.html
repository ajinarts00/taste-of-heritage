Tradish — Brunei

Five Brunei dishes with full steps.

← Back

Ambuyat

Starchy sago-based staple eaten with dips.

Steps

  1. Boil water; whisk in sago starch until glue-like paste forms; stir until smooth and serve with dips.

Nasi Katok

Steamed rice with fried chicken and sambal.

Steps

  1. Steam rice; fry chicken; assemble with sambal and cucumber.

Hinava

Fresh fish salad marinated in lime juice.

Steps

  1. Cube fish; marinate in lime until opaque; add shallots and chilies; season and serve.

Soto Brunei

Turmeric chicken soup with noodles.

Steps

  1. Simmer chicken with turmeric and ginger; shred and serve over noodles with sprouts.

Keropok Lekor

Fish cracker snack — boiled then fried.

Steps

  1. Mix fish paste with tapioca flour, shape into rolls, boil then deep-fry until crisp.
Serve with chili sauce.
