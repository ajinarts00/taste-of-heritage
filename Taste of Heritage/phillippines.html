Adobo

Savory-sour stew of chicken or pork with soy & vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 bay leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate protein in soy & vinegar with garlic for 30 min.
  2. Simmer with marinade, add bay leaves until reduced and tender.
Serve hot with steamed rice.

Sinigang

Sour tamarind soup with vegetables and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork or shrimp
  • 200 g tamarind paste or soup mix
  • Assorted vegetables (radish, kangkong, eggplant)

Steps

  1. Boil protein to make broth; add tamarind for sourness.
  2. Add vegetables and simmer until cooked.

Lechon Kawali

Crispy deep-fried pork belly.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • Salt, pepper
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Boil pork with aromatics until tender; chill thoroughly.
  2. Deep-fry until crackling crisp.
Slice and serve with dipping sauce.

Pancit Canton

Stir-fried egg noodles with veggies and protein.

Ingredients

  • 300 g egg noodles
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Chicken or pork slices

Steps

  1. Stir-fry protein and vegetables, add noodles and season with soy.

Halo-Halo

Layered shaved-ice dessert with sweet beans, fruit & leche flan.

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice
  • Assorted sweetened beans and fruit
  • Evaporated milk
  • Leche flan or ube halaya

Steps

  1. Layer ingredients in a tall glass, top with shaved ice and milk, garnish with flan.
Serve with spoon—mix before eating.
