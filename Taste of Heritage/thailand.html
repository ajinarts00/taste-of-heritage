Pad Thai

Stir-fried rice noodles with tamarind, egg, tofu & shrimp.

Ingredients (2–3)

  • 200 g rice noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g shrimp
  • 100 g tofu
  • 3 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Soak noodles until pliable. Mix tamarind, fish sauce and sugar as sauce.
  2. Heat wok, fry tofu and shrimp until cooked; add noodles and sauce; toss quickly.
  3. Push aside, scramble eggs, mix with noodles. Serve with crushed peanuts and lime.
Serve immediately with lime and crushed peanuts.

Tom Yum Goong

Hot & sour shrimp soup with lemongrass, galangal & kaffir lime.

Ingredients (4)

  • 400 g shrimp
  • 3 stalks lemongrass
  • 4 slices galangal
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2–3 bird’s eye chilies
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • Juice of 2 limes

Steps

  1. Crush aromatics lightly and simmer in water to make broth.
  2. Add shrimp and cook until pink; season with fish sauce and lime juice.
  3. Finish with chilies and serve hot.
Serve hot as starter or main with rice.

Green Curry

Fragrant coconut curry with green curry paste, eggplant & basil.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 300 g chicken or tofu
  • 1 eggplant
  • Thai basil leaves

Steps

  1. Fry curry paste; add coconut milk and simmer.
  2. Add chicken and vegetables; cook until tender. Finish with basil.
Serve with steamed rice.

Som Tum (Papaya Salad)

Crunchy pounded green papaya salad with chilies & lime.

Ingredients

  • 1 small green papaya, shredded
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2–3 chilies
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • Peanuts to garnish

Steps

  1. Pound garlic & chilies lightly; add sugar, lime and fish sauce.
  2. Add shredded papaya and pound lightly to bruise and mix flavors.
  3. Serve topped with peanuts and sliced long beans.
Serve fresh as a side or light meal.

Mango Sticky Rice

Sweet coconut sticky rice paired with ripe mango.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 ripe mangoes

Steps

  1. Soak rice, steam until soft.
  2. Heat coconut milk with sugar; mix into cooked rice and rest.
  3. Slice mango and serve with coconut sticky rice.
Serve at room temperature or slightly warm.
